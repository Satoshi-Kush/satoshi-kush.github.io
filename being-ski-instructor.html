Being a Ski Instructor in Catalonia

Yeah I know it is strange, but I'm an ski instructor in SPAIN.
Since I was a youngster I've loved the mountains, and specially when they are white. I started skiing when I was around 3 years old, and even though I did not like it for the first 2-3 years, I'm so glad my dad kept me pushing to learn how to be better at it. To a point that I'm the one who teaches now how to ski!
During my teen years I spent almost every weekend going to the mountains, and getting as good as I could skiing. It meant so much for me, that I would not be the one I am today without it. And the day I made 16 years old, I decided I had to bring that energy and passion to the next generations, as skiing is not just a sport for me, it means freedom, liberty and peace of mind, and so I wanted to teach young kids the same.


I've always worked at InterCerdanya - Camp Base Escola d'Esquí Llívia, as there is where I grew up, and I felt part of them.
Nowadays I'm not working anymore, as I'm just enjoying again this beautiful sport, and learning new things to improve and push myself to new limits.

However, in case you want to learn how to ski, and live near Barcelona (or know someone who wants to), contact me, and we can arrange some ski lessons without problem! I've always loved seeing people thrive as they learn how to ski, and I'll do everything I can to help you go down the slopes as fast & stylish as possible!
Contact me!
