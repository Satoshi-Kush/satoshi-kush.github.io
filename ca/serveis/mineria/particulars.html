Easy Teaching, Easy Learning

Don't you have the feeling that Covid-19 showed to the world the huge number of flaws that the current educational system has?
Since March 2020, we have felt that way, and that's why, along 5 of my colleagues, we started iLern Education.
A platform designed by students, specifically focused on Teachers and Students needs, not just corporation and office needs. Want to know more about iLern, what do we do, and how do we do it? Take a look at our website (I'll let you the link below) and create an account to try the first online education platform, designed for you and your team mates, to work together as easy as possible.

With a high security inside your classroom, many features to collaborate, and without the need to download anything.
