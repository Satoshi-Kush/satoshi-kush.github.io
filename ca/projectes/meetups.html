Coinstreet Limited

One of the things that helped me improve my trading so much has been being part of the Coinstreet Trading Club.
Coinstreet is a Trading Club, focused on Bitcoin markets, where you can learn different strategies and apply them to the market you want.
Once you get inside the trading world, there are many, MANY scams out there waiting for people who are looking for quick and easy returns, and the truth is that trading is EXACTLY the opposite.
Trading is about Risk Management, Emotions Control and developing a strategy that works for you, and following it strictly and consistently. Nothing about quick and easy returns, quite exactly the other way round.
That's why I decided to stay in Coinstreet, the only group that helped me improve in my trading. There they do not send ANY Trading signals, to try to get more and more followers. There you'll just learn how to analyse the market and take your decisions by yourself. I've met some of my mentors there, and quickly you distinguish between someone who knows what trading is, and can really teach you, and someone who just wants to get your money.

In case you are interested in trading, and want to know more about it, either if you are just starting, or you are a seasoned trader, Coinstreet is a perfect group to meet other traders and to keep learning and sharing the information to succeed in the markets.
